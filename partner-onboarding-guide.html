Partner Onboarding Guide

  • Home
  • Partner Onboarding Guide

Who are we?

  • We are a technology platform that connects customers with on demand logistics services
  • The service of our company is to match customers with logistics patners best suited to deliver service to them through the use of our range of web and mobile applications either downloaded, installed and/or accessed via our website.

Why join Airand?

  • Steady stream of customers
  • Zero marketing
  • Maximise your ROI with orders on reverse trips
  • Tailored orders that best suit your dispatchers realtime location and cargo capacity

How it works

  • Register on our platform on partners.airand.net
  • Add your dispatcher(s) details
  • Get your dispatcher(s) to download our app
  • Your dispatcher(s) get orders closest to their realtime location
  • Your dispatch manager can also accept or decline delivery requests sent as links via email and sms on behalf of your dispatcher just incase he/she cannot at that moment
  • Once a delivery request is accepted either by your dispatcher or manager, your manager coordinates with your dispatcher to ensure value is rendered to the customer accurately and promptly with the details provided.
  • On the Airand Pilot App, your dispatcher(s) should check to ensure that payment has been made via our platform before completing a delivery
  • Settlement is done on a weekly basis with a commision of just 10%

Documents (accurate and up to date)

  • CAC Certificate (Businesses)/ Valid ID (Individuals)
  • Vehicle license
  • Driver's license
  • Stage carrier permit
  • Nipost license
  • Road Worthiness
  • Motor insurance

PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) and device

  • GPS enabled Android smart phone
  • Helmet for motorcycles
  • Knee and elbow pads
  • Protective cover shoes

Payment Policy

  • Logistics partners and their personnel are prohibited from requesting direct payment from customers
  • Logistics partners and their personnel are only permitted to demand payment be made on our platform via the payment link(s) sent to the customer by Airand.
  • Logistics partners are paid via fund transfers into the bank account provided in the registration form
  • All outstanding payments are made on a weekly basis
  • Airand charges a 10% commission on ever transaction
  • All pending commissions are deducted before fund transfers are done

Step by step Registration

  1. Log on to partners.airand.net
  2. Enter your preferred email and password and click 'Sign up'
  3. Click on the profile picture icon, click update and fill in all your company details
  4. Click on the bike icon and add dispatch riders

Adding dispatchers

NB: Only dispatcher phone numbers added on your portal can receive sms OTPs

  1. Your dispatchers are required to download and install the "Airandrider" App on Google Playstore
  2. Enter their respective phone numbers as in the company registration
  3. A 6-digit OTP will be sent to the mobile number via text. Enter the 6-digit code received
  4. Accept all app permissions
  5. Now your dispatcher is ready to receive orders closest to him for pickup and delivery
