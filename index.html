Become a delivery partner in 3 easy steps

Step 1) Register on
Admin portal

Fill in all company details, dispatch manager details and rider details.

The Admin portal enables dispatch managers to manage all operations and personnel from one portal conveniently.

Monitor rider status, earnings and periodical performance.

Register now

Step 2) Download Rider App on rider(s) phones

Get your dispatcher(s) to download the app on Google playstore.

Each rider would enter their phone number as filled in the portal to receive a login OTP code, accept all permissions and they're good to go.

NB: Rider App must always run in background for rider(s) to receive dispatch requests.

Step 3) Up and Running

Now you can accept dispatch requests as a manager on behalf of your rider.

Click on the link sent to you via SMS to accept or decline.
